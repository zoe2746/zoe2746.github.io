Summer Design Internship at AI Solly

Product Introduction
AI SOLLY is a virtual social companion designed for students with special needs. It provides a variety of real-life school scenarios in which Solly, a virtual classmate, interacts with the child. The system uses AI to generate personalized responses that guide students through practicing social communication. Through repeated practice, learners gradually build positive communication and social-interaction skills.

Beyond being a helpful assistant during class, AI SOLLY also integrates teaching management tools, SEL (Social Emotional Learning) curriculum generation, and student learning-progress tracking, making it a comprehensive platform for educators.
