DESIGN AND GRAPHIC LANGUAGES STUDIO

We are requested to produce two booklets in this studio, and this is the first.
Since Italy has the best gelato in the world, I made it my subject of first booklet.

The booklet dig into gelato in various topics: history, difference between gelato and ice cream, recipe, and concluding with a personal guide to Rome's best (and worst) gelato. Pages with empty card and included to encourage reader to explore and establish their own guide.
BOOK DESIGN AND TYPOGRAPHY STUDIO

In this studio, we are asked to design a font inspired by a singer of our choice.
This typeface is inspired by BIBI, a Korean singer known for her bold style and raw emotions.
It captures her mix of sweetness and edge, blending softness with attitude.
With a strong skeleton, we curve the once angular shape into a playful, candy-like font.
